Text Classification

Select a tab below to classify either your own custom text or the latest tweets from a Twitter user. The function will break down your text and explain why it was classified as belonging to a given school. Please be patient as it can take a good amount of time, especially for larger texts.

This feature uses a Multinomial Naive Bayes classifier to classify input text into one of the schools in the dataset. This enables a word-by-word breakdown of why the model chose the school that it did. For more information on how the model works, see our page on interpreting the results.
