Text Statistics

Enter a school, author, or title to get basic stats on that source.

Note that the both the frequency chart and the bigram chart do not include stopwords such as 'you' or 'the.' The bigram chart counts as phrases pairs of words connected by stopwords - so 'unity, apperception' could be 'the unity of apperception,' for example.
